Bootstrap automatically styles all elements with <input>, <textarea>, and < select> with .form-control class has a width of 100%.

The three types of form layouts are Veritcal (default), horizontal, and inline.

ALWAYS:

  • Always use <form role="form"> to help people who use screen readers.
  • Wrap labels and form controls in <div class="form-group"> to give it optimum spacing
  • Add .form-control class to all <input>, <textarea>, and <select> elements.
