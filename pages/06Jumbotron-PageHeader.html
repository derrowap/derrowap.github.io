Jumbotron Inside Container

Placing the jumbotron inside a container forces it to NOT extend to the edge of the screen.

Jumbotron Outside Container

Placing the jumbotron outside the container forces it to extend to the edge of the screen.

This is a Page Header!!

Page headers act like a section divider. It adds a horizontal line under the heading and adds some extra space around the element.
